About Us

We have a thorough understanding of project management, contract administration, employer’s agent, quantity surveying and clerk of works.

We support our clients to manage their projects more effectively, deliver at pace, achieve best value for money, mitigate risk and identify opportunities for value engineering.

Our combined mix of Client side, Consultancy and Contractor experience has given us the opportunity to have all hats over some competitors in that we understand the requirements and obligations of all parties. Our work with Local Authorities and Housing Associations means we are constantly in touch with the market and live market rates; thus allowing us to provide them with accurate programme, early cost advice, accurate pre-tender estimation and quality contract administration. We specialise in all forms of JCT and NEC contracts.

Our quality end-to-end services from start throughout the end of defect liability period have earned us a reputation for success.

Our Construction Consultancy Services support delivery of each contract, providing experienced employer’s agent, project management, contract administration, cost management and clerk of works to meet your project needs.
